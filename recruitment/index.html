Greetings fromWiOS Chapter

We hope all of you are doing well. Are you all looking for a doorway to energise yourselves, to indulge in creative endeavours by joining a vibrant team? Brace yourselves as now you have got the chance to be one among us. WiOS is here to invite all fresh minds with original and passionate ideas to join our community for the academic year 2023-2024.

 
 

WiOS chapter promotes gender diversity in technology and encourages the use of open-source frameworks.

We hold hands-on workshops, plan hackathons and seminars where you can learn about and experiment with different open-source technologies, compete with others who share your interests, and more.


Additionally, our partnerships with other chapters and organisations at the university will provide our club members with a variety of experiences in a wide range of domains.
